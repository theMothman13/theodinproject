Cowboy Soup

Dish Description

Memories of this soup include bringing constant sibling rivalry to a quiet peace on the rare occasions it was made, so it holds a special place in my wife's heart. It's filling, it heals, and it's good soup.

Ingredients

  • roast beef
  • cabbage
  • onion
  • carrot
  • garlic
  • celery
  • potatoes
  • diced tomatoes - 1 can
  • corn - 1 can
  • beef broth
  • thyme
  • rosemary
  • oregano
  • black pepper
  • sea salt

Steps

  1. Cook your roast, then shred the meat for preparation.
  2. Cut up your veggies however you prefer consuming them.
  3. Roast your veggies in butter or oil.
  4. Bring your broth to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer.
  5. Start with your potatoes, then mix in the rest of your vegetables.
  6. Season the crap out your soup at this point, letting the seasonings absorb into your ingredients. After 20 minutes of simmering, put in the meat and continue to simmer.
  7. Serve up and enjoy the silence good food brings!
